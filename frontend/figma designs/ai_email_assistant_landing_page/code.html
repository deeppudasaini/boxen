Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We've got answers. If you can't find what you're looking for, feel free to contact us.

How does the AI understand email context?

expand_more
Our AI uses advanced Natural Language Processing (NLP) models trained on vast datasets of conversational data. It analyzes not just keywords, but the relationships between words, sentence structure, and historical context within a thread to accurately determine intent, sentiment, and key topics.

Is my data secure?

expand_more
Yes, data security is our top priority. We use end-to-end encryption for all data in transit and at rest. Your email content is processed securely and is never used for training models without your explicit consent.

What email providers do you support?

expand_more
We support all major email providers, including Gmail, Outlook, and any provider that allows for IMAP access. Our setup process is simple and guides you through connecting your account in just a few clicks.

Can I cancel my subscription at any time?

expand_more
Absolutely. You can cancel your subscription at any time directly from your account dashboard. There are no hidden fees or long-term contracts. You'll retain access until the end of your current billing period.
